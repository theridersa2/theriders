The Riders Collective

We are a community of riders in and around Ann Arbor + Ypsilanti dedicated to making transit comfortable, convenient, and joyful!

Check out some of our events below. You can contact us at theridersa2@gmail.com.

Transit Happy Hour

Join other riders as we practice civic engagement. TheRide Board meetings are open to the public, but are rarely attended.

Come share your story about how the bus affects your life, for good and bad, or just come and listen to what our transit agency is working on.

Then hang out with your fellow riders to debrief. It's a great way to meet fellow transit nerds.

Hope to see you at the next one!
